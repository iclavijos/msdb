To import this calendar to your Google Calendar account, you just need to perform a simple step:

  • Click on the button you can see below and you will be automatically redirected, in a
    different window, to Google Calendar. There you will be able to confirm the addition
    of the calendar
Once you complete that step, you will start receiving any updates made on this page in your calendar.
Beware: Synchronization is not automatic and a few hours may be needed for the changes to be
displayed in your calendar.
